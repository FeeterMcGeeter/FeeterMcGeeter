Born, but not raised!

I was originally an Indiana Pacers fan because of Reggie Miller, but after he retired, they were pretty bad. They were'nt on TV as much, so I couldn't watch them living in the DFW area. I ended up following and rooting for the Mavericks. The Texas Rangers have been the only team in Texas I've been a fan of since day one.

I have also been bowling for over 20 years now. I'd probably put myself in the "above average bowler" category. Not great, but above average. I only have one perfect game in my 20 plus years, which is why I'd only say I'm above average.
